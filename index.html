FAQ

Common questions.

Does SummarAI summarize my text for me? +
No - and that's the point. SummarAI is a learning tool, not a shortcut. You write the summary yourself, and the AI gives you feedback on how well you understood the material.
Is SummarAI really free? +
Yes, completely free. No credit card, no subscription, no hidden fees. Just create an account and start using it.
What kind of text can I use? +
Anything - articles, textbook chapters, essays, news stories, lecture notes. You can paste text directly or upload a text-based PDF. Any subject, any topic.
Do I need to download an app? +
No. SummarAI runs entirely in your browser. Open the link on your phone, tablet, or computer and it works instantly.
Are my sessions saved? +
Yes. Every session - your text, summary, AI feedback score, and quiz results - is saved to your account. You can review your history and track your progress over time.
Who is SummarAI for? +
Anyone who reads to learn. Students, professionals, self-learners - if you want to actually understand and remember what you read, SummarAI is for you.
